About Us

Making workflow in teams more easy and more convinient.

TeamGlade is simple and easy to use online place for collaboration in teams or students groups. Our platform allows teams to create workspaces, where members can share files, exchange messages, and collaborate in real-time.

The main ‘thrust’ is to make workflow simple and convenient. Whether you're working on a small project or managing a large team, our platform empowers you to communicate efficiently and effortlessly.
